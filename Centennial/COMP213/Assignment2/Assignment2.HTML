Websites about Protected Areas Issues

   A protected area is a clearly defined geographical space, recognised, dedicated and managed, through legal or other effective means, to achieve the long term conservation of nature with associated ecosystem services and cultural values. It allows existing species that are close to extinction to live longer and protects them from hunters that try to kill them for materials (fur, skin, bones, etc.). Not only do protected areas secure biodiversity conservation, they also secure the well-being of humanity itself.
