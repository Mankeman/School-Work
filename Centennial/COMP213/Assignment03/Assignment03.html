Samsung Galaxy s10

After switching from Apple products to Samsung products, you'll never want to switch to another company again. The Galaxy S10 is a fitting 10th anniversary phone for Samsung and its stored S series. It delivers on change with a novel-looking Infinity-O screen so large it displaces the front camera, and a triple-lens rear camera that takes ultra-wide photos. Its in-screen fingerprint sensor tech should serve you well, while its Wireless PowerShare could serve your friends well. That’s a lot of change – just know that it comes at a high price and the Galaxy S10e and S10 Plus flank it from both sides of the coin as better options.
